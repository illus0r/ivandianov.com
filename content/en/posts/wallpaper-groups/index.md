The 17 wallpaper groups are all possible ways to tile a plane with a repeating pattern. Each group has a unique combination of symmetries: translations, rotations, reflections, and glide reflections.

Here I collect shader implementations of each group — minimal code that demonstrates the symmetry.

## Groups

- [*442 (p4m)](/posts/wallpaper-groups/p4m/) — square lattice with 4-fold rotation and mirror lines
