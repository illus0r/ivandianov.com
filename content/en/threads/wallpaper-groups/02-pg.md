Glide reflections only — no rotations, no pure reflections. Each row is the previous one flipped and shifted by half a cell.

::: glsl
vec2 uv=(FC.xy*2.-r)/min(r.x,r.y)*2.5;
uv.x=fract(uv.x);
if(mod(floor(uv.y),2.)==0.)uv.x=1.-uv.x;
uv.y=fract(uv.y);
vec3 a=vec3(0,.85,.5),b=vec3(.9,0,.6),c=vec3(.95,.85,0);
o.rgb+=mix(mix(a,b,uv.x),c,uv.y*1.4);
:::
