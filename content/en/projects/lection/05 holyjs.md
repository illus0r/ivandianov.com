# Talk at HolyJS: Algorithmic Graphics: JS for Design and Art

I gave this talk at HolyJS. Showed several of the coolest generative projects and explained the techniques and algorithms I used for each one.

There was a lot of new and interesting things: new people, locations. And tools. For example, I made the presentation in sli.dev, here's the [log chat about the process](https://t.me/ivandianov_log/649)
